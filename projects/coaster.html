CNC-Machined Coaster

Status: Complete

Summary: A compact CNC milling project designed to demonstrate clean CAD/CAM execution and machining fundamentals.

Overview

This project was intentionally constrained. The goal was to show solid command of the full CNC workflow, from CAD through CAM to a finished part, with clear toolpath sequencing and attention to finish.

The CAM includes a range of common operations in one part: engraving, pocketing, chamfering, and finishing passes.

Tools and skills

CAD CAM CNC milling Setup discipline

What I did

  • Designed a coaster to hold my favorite morning beverage, engraved with logos and sized for a medium iced coffee cup
  • Created a CAM file to machine the coaster
  • Machined it out of HDPE on a CNC mill
