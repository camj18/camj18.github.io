VICE: Vibram Impact Cushioning Evaluator

Status: Prototype 1

Summary: I worked with a team, alongside Vibram, to design, manufacture, and test a test rig to analyze the energy returned to their shoes after each step.

Overview

The testing rig was designed so that a shoe can be placed in a holder at the end of a horizontal pendulum. When the pendulum is released, the shoe hits a rigid impact plate, then bounces back. We use the ratio between the starting and ending height of the pendulum to determine the energy return ratio.

Tools and skills

CAD CAM Test Rig Design Simulations

What I did

  • Designed all components for the current prototype in Fusion
  • Ran static stress simulations on the latch
  • Ran dynamic stress simulations on the impact plate
  • Helped manufacture the scaled down prototype
