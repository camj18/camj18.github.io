Generative Design Post (2 kN Load, Minimum Mass)

Status: Complete

Summary: Used generative design to create a 3D‑printed post that meets a 2 kN load requirement with minimal mass, comparing results across safety factors.

Overview

The goal of this project was to design and print a post that could hold a 2 kN load without deforming to a height of less than 50 mm. To do this, I used Fusion 360’s generative design mode. I set the constraints to match the project parameters and ran three different designs at safety factors of 1, 1.5, and 2, respectively. Since the design with a safety factor of 1 had the lowest mass, I used it for the final test. The post I used passed the test, not deforming to under 50 mm under a 2 kN load.

What I did
