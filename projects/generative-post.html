Generative Design Post (2 kN Load, Minimum Mass)

Status: Complete

Summary: Used generative design to create a 3D-printed post that meets a 2 kN load requirement with minimal mass, comparing results across safety factors.

Overview

This project focused on converting a requirement into constraints and evaluating outputs logically, rather than selecting a visually interesting shape.

I used Fusion 360 generative design across multiple safety factors to show the robustness versus mass trade space.

Tools and skills

CAD Generative design Constraints Validation mindset
Expand details constraints • selection • validation
  • Requirement: 2 kN load capacity under defined boundary conditions.
  • Exploration: safety factors of 1, 1.5, and 2 to compare robustness vs mass.
  • Selection: representative designs chosen to explain the trade space.

Next iteration would include a formal assumption sheet (loads, constraints, material properties, print orientation) plus a simple verification step.
