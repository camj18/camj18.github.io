Magic Rings (4-Axis Machining Illusion)

Status: Complete

Summary: Planned and executed a multi‑axis machining strategy to create two interlocked rings with a seamless “impossible” appearance.

Overview

The goal of this project was to create a necklace that had two interlocking stainless steel rings with no seams or gaps from when they were attached. This meant I had to machine the rings in a way in which they would already be interlocked. Through a lot of optimization in CAM, I decided to machine it on a 4‑axis CNC mill and leave a small tab connecting the rings which could be manually removed after. I also decided to shape it by hand to give it a more hand-made feel.

Tools and skills

CAD 4-axis CAM Fixturing Hand finishing

What I did

  • Created a CAD design of the rings
  • Created a 4‑axis CAM procedure to machine the rings
  • Machined them on a 4‑axis CNC mill
  • Used hand tools to shape the rings
